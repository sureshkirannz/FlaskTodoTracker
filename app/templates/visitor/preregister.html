Preregistration Information

When you preregister a visitor:

  • An email invitation will be sent to the visitor with check-in instructions
  • The host will be notified of the scheduled visit
  • Visitor will have a streamlined check-in experience when they arrive
  • The system will send a reminder to both the visitor and host before the scheduled visit
Need to send documents for review?

You can attach documents like NDAs or safety policies to be reviewed and signed prior to the visit.
